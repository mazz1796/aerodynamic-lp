The man behind the scene

Koji Hiramatsu

Founder

I am a massive car enthusiast. It was about four years old when I drove an electric go kart in the small amusement park where my parents toook me. Ever since I am being fascinated by cars. I was born and raised in Japan. I moved to Melbourne 2 years ago and I really appreciate being the part of this amazing country. But there is only one complaint to make as a petrolhead. There are many car manufacturers in Japan and the market is very competitive. So cars are generally much cheaper to buy in Japan than Australia. I have been wondering if there is any way to get around thing problem and came up with this car sharing service. Hope you can enjoy awesome cars and driving them.
